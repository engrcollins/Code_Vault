About Whack-a-Mole!

Whack-a-mole is a popular amusement arcade game invented in 1976 by Aaron Fechter of Creative Engineering, Inc. To play the game, use a mallet(mouse-click) to hit toy moles, which appear randomly, back into their holes. Each hit gives you 1 point while each miss gives no point. You have 20 seconds to hit as many moles as possible.
